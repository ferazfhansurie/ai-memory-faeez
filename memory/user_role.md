The user (Firaz / ferazfhansurie) is the founder of **ADletic Agency** and the builder of **MotionBoards** — an AI media canvas product (Next.js 16 / React 19 / Tailwind v4 / shadcn) where users generate images, videos, and audio across 30+ models and stitch them on a board.

**Day-to-day creative practice:**
- Writes treatments and prompts for short-form ad spots (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts), often for Malaysian small-business clients (e.g. ESP Wrapz car detailing)
- Produces directly through MotionBoards using **Seedance 2.0 I2V/S2E** for video, **Nano Banana 2** for keyframes/staging, plus Veo, Sora, Wan, etc.
- Uses **real photos for character identity** — does not generate characters from scratch with NB2 (a deliberate choice to avoid licensing greyzone and identity drift)
- Localised content: Malaysian wardrobe (e.g. JDT football jerseys, hijab/non-hijab pairings), KL/JB street settings, golden-hour mood

**Inspiration / aesthetic reference:** **Yaro Flasher** (founder of Motion Design School, builder of FlashBoards — a similar Creative AI Canvas product). His public prompting style (long single-prompt multi-reference Seedance runs, real-photo character locks, paste-ready ASCII) is a direct reference for how Firaz wants to work.

**How to collaborate:**
- Treat him as a senior creative + senior engineer simultaneously — no need to over-explain either side
- He prefers long-form treatments AND a paste-ready compressed version (Seedance has a 2000-char ceiling on most upstream wrappers)
- For code in MotionBoards, he wants surgical edits with verified facts (e.g. cite source for any model character limit before shipping)
- Bias toward shipping over planning; he'll course-correct fast
