## 1. The look (global style — reuse this block on every shot)
> Mixed-media animation in the spirit of The Amazing World of Gumball and Smiling Friends: bold 2D hand-drawn cel-shaded cartoon characters (thick confident outlines, simple shading, normal eyes) composited into richly detailed, photoreal real-world Malaysian environments - real textures, real grime, real practical lighting. The deliberate clash between the flat cartoon characters and the photographic, textured world is the whole look. Characters stay hand-drawn; backgrounds stay real and detailed, never flat.

**Language rule (all dialogue):** natural casual **Kuala Lumpur Malaysian accent, relaxed urban KL Manglish**, final "a" softened to a schwa, **NOT Indonesian** (no Indonesian intonation/vocabulary).

**World:** night-time KL — kopitiam back-alleys, roadside burger stall, concrete monsoon drains (longkang), and the crew's HQ inside a beat-up **teal box-lorry**.

## 2. Cast bible (exact identity refs)
- **Kit** — black bucket hat, dark teal coveralls with silver reflective bands, black gloves. The used-cooking-oil collector hero; cheeky, exasperated comic lead, breaks the 4th wall.
- **D.R. Up** — swept black hair, white lab coat over green turtleneck, black gloves. Serene mentor/leader; cryptic calm wisdom ("Setiap titik… ade harapan").
- **Sparron** — blonde wavy hair, glowing green AR visor, black top, green pants. Deadpan tech operator at the monitor.
- **Glinciro** *(villain)* — white boater hat, thin beard, white shirt with black vest. The illegal "gutter-oil" lowballer who lurks in storm drains (Pennywise/IT parody), luring people to sell used oil cheap.
- **Aiman** — tired late-20s Malaysian man, plain t-shirt + flat cap. Struggling roadside burger-stall owner drowning in debt (PTPTN) — the vulnerable vendor.

## 3. Pipeline / models used
- **Start-frame stills:** `gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview` (Nano Banana) — cartoon-character-over-photoreal composites, using each character's identity image as reference.
- **Animation (I2V + generated dialogue/audio):** `dreamina-seedance-2-0-260128/omni` and `…-fast-260128/omni` (Seedance 2.0, speaks the dialogue); some shots via `veo-3.1-fast-generate-preview/i2v`.
