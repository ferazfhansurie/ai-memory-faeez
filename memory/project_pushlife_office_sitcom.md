**PUSH Life** is a single-camera mockumentary comedy series (The Office / Parks & Rec format) for FatHopes Energy's **PUSH program** — the used-cooking-oil (UCO) collection arm (mini-tanker entrepreneurs + depot staff). FatHopes turns UCO into Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF); see [[user_role]].

**Why it works / the tone (this is the brief):** comedy comes from treating a deeply mundane, smelly job (collecting old cooking oil) with total corporate gravity ("we are decarbonising aviation"). Dry talking-head confessionals, awkward pauses, real Manglish, heart underneath. NOT generic "Gen-Z vs boss" TikTok skits — those read CRINGE. The humour MUST be grounded in the real UCO world (oil drums, restaurant routes, mini-tanker, Loyalty App leaderboard, tonnage KPIs, "Hari Kita Semula", SAF, mamak pickups, RM5000/month PUSH ad). Specificity = funny. Follow [[feedback_content_genz_funny_serious]] but skew to Office-realism over trend-jacking.

**Cast (archetypes → UCO world):**
- **Boss / Depot Manager** (Michael Scott): thinks he's personally saving aviation; speeches over oil drums; obsessed with the Loyalty App leaderboard + monthly tonnage; quotes "Hari Kita Semula."
- **Abang [veteran collector]** (Dwight): 15 yrs, routes are sacred territory, grades oil by smell, distrusts the app.
- **Budak baru** (the Gen-Z new hire): AirPods, unbothered, calls the mini-tanker "the whip", hits target without trying.
- **Senior driver** (Jim): deadpan, glances at camera, stuck partnering the kid.
- **Kak [admin / app coordinator]** (Pam): actually runs the place, fields angry restaurant calls.

**Production:** real people / real depot (Ara Damansara shoot planned), lo-fi single-cam, Manglish. Shoot day = half-day cast+script lock, half-day shoot.
